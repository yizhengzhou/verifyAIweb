How to Spot Photos Used in Romance Scams

Quick answer: Search the photos for prior appearances, then check whether the names, locations, occupations, and timeline match the profile’s claims.

Why this search matters

Romance scams use emotional trust and often reuse attractive portraits, military photos, professional headshots, or lifestyle images.

How to use VerifyAI

  1. Choose a clear face photo and one lifestyle photo if available.
  2. Run each image separately in VerifyAI.
  3. Inspect exact matches, stock-photo results, public-figure alerts, and pages using different names.
  4. Save evidence, stop payments, and report the account through the platform if the story does not add up.

What the result cannot prove

AI-image detection is only a reference signal. Even a real photo can be operated by a scammer who stole it.
