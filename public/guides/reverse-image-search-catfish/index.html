Reverse Image Search for Catfish Checks

Quick answer: Use reverse image search to see whether a dating profile photo was copied from another person, a public account, or a stock-photo source.

Why this search matters

Catfish accounts often depend on borrowed images and avoid real-time verification. Image search is most useful when combined with behavior and direct verification.

How to use VerifyAI

  1. Search more than one profile photo; scammers may mix stolen and original-looking images.
  2. Use VerifyAI to compare results from Google, Yandex, Google Lens, and Shutterstock.
  3. Record source links and discrepancies instead of confronting the person immediately.
  4. If money, investment, emergencies, or secrecy enter the conversation, stop and speak with someone you trust.

What the result cannot prove

A clean search is not a clean bill of health. Treat urgency, isolation, payment requests, and refusal to video chat as separate warning signs.
