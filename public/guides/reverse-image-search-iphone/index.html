How to Reverse Image Search on iPhone

Quick answer: Save or screenshot the image, open VerifyAI, choose the photo, and run a multi-engine search. Review matching pages rather than relying on a single score.

Why this search matters

This is useful when an image appears in a dating profile, marketplace listing, message, or social post and you want to find where else it has appeared.

How to use VerifyAI

  1. Save the clearest available version of the image. Avoid screenshots with large borders when possible.
  2. Open VerifyAI and select the image from Photos or share it directly to the app.
  3. Run the full search across Google, Yandex, Google Lens, and Shutterstock.
  4. Open likely matches and compare dates, names, captions, crops, and the original page context.

What the result cannot prove

No match does not prove an image or profile is authentic. The image may be new, private, altered, or not indexed.
